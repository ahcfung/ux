Hello there! I'm Alex. Right now, I'm working towards a B.S. in Cognitive Science with specialization in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of California, San Diego and will be able to work full-time starting this June.

I live in the realm of user experience, where people's intents, goals and mental models drive design. My main interest gears toward the subfield of user experience research, but I also enjoy diving into the actual conceptualization phase of designing and prototyping. To me, designs should not simply look good. They should also work in a way that makes a difference in users' daily lives–whether it be through how the design communicates to the users, the possibilities that the design unlocks, or other innovative means.

If you want to know more about my work, feel free to contact me!
