Hello there! I'm Alex. Right now, I'm working towards a B.S. in Cognitive Science with specialization in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of California, San Diego.

I live in the realm of user experience, but am particularly fascinated by user experience research. Through my projects, I have enjoyed many aspects of this subfield--from talking with participants in order to gather useful insights to learning about their difficulties as they speak aloud their thoughts in usability tests. Despite my preference in this subfield, I don't mind getting some hands-on work with design since engaging myself in other roles will only help me improve.

If you want to know more about my work, feel free to contact me!
