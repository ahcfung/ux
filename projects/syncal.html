SynCal

Portfolio > Syncal

Overview

Currently, the calendars for the UC San Diego community are scattered in different forms and places. In light of this, my team and I spent 10 weeks to design a uniform UC San Diego Calendar interface.

Methods

To first understand the target user groups, we conducted user interviews with 37 participants. After deriving insights from our interviews, we discovered common themes by building an affinity diagram. We then converted the findings into personas and use-case scenarios that drove forward our design prototype.

Solution

The final prototype is not simply an aggregated, online calendar, but it is a means to increase event awareness and encourage event attendance. Using this interface, event creators can share events with specific groups or people using multiple methods. Integrating Facebook into the interface, students receive tailored event suggestions based on which events their friends are attending.
