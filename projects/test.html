Attorney Website

Portfolio > Attorney Website

Overview

Many attorneys and firms have websites to advertise their presence to the public. To keep up with his competitors, a sole practitioner approached my team. He requested a website that will attract more clients while promoting his presence online.

Methods

We began the project by surveying the client to establish his target audience and goals for the website. With the target audience established, we interviewed users that closely resemble the target audience to understand their needs. We converted the interview data into user personas to better represent each target user population. To ensure our client's website meets his competitors' standards, we conducted a competitive analysis of three attorney websites. After storyboarding our ideas, I helped the team with implementing the design.

Solution

After several iterations, we created a website prototype that fulfills the client's and his target users' needs. Some notable features include an omnipresent contact form that encourages potential users to reach out to our client. Additionally, to support users who are unfamiliar with law and to establish trust for our client, we created a process page to explain the client's work process and a testimonial page to showcase success stories.
