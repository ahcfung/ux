Wine Cellar

Portfolio > Wine Cellar

Overview

The final class project for Cogs187A: Cognitive Aspects of Digital Design was to design an ezine that consolidates online articles for a topic. My team and I decided to design our ezine on the topic of wine.

Methods

We conducted interviews with wine consumers of different expertise, then converted their needs into secondary navigational labels for the site's information architecture. We made use of a content inventory to organize our efforts in consolidating relevant web pages and other content for the site. Simultaneously, I converted our low-fidelity sketches into high-fidelity wireframes using Photoshop.

Solution

Ultimately, we developed an ezine that fulfills the needs of novice, causal and expert wine consumers. Within some of the navigation labels, there are web articles that provide the same information, but in differing depths based on the needs of different user groups.
